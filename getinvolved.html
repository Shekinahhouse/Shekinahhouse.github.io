Offering hope and providing services and support for the homeless

Volunteer Opportunities

Concierge

Meet and greet visitors to our premises, minimum commitment of 3 hours per week.

Fundraising Team

Join our amazing team to organise and volunteer at events.

Shower and Laundry Services

Help us to maintain these vital services - minimum commitment of 3 hours a week.

Sunday evening meals

Volunteer to help set up and pack up the dining room and serve the three course evening meal - commitment of 2 hours on a roster basis.

Volunteer to cook the main course or provide a dessert.

Are you a business or organisation that wants to get involved?

  • Ask us to attend your workplace or group to share about homelessness and ways you can support us.
  • Hold a fundraising event - your financial support helps us to help others
  • Would you like one of our donation boxes to place on your shop counter?

Donate

Tax deductible donations

All donations of $2 or more are tax deductible. Whether you give a one-off donation or commit to a regular monthly donation, know that you are ensuring that we can continue to meet the need of some of the most vulnerable and disadvantaged among us.
