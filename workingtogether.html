Working Together - Collaboration

Offering hope and providing services and support for the homeless

Shekinah House is one of a number of organisations working collaboratively with those who are homeless or at risk of homelessness.

We are a member of the City of Launceston's Homeless Advisory Group and also a member of the Northern Community Action Group (for homelessness).

We work collaboratively with organisations such as (but not limited to):

  • Prison Fellowship Australia (Tasmania) Prison Fellowship
  • Housing Connect Housing Connect - Anglicare
  • Homelessness Outreach Workers from other organisations such as:
    • Salvation Army Launceston Corps | The Salvation Army Australia
    • Launceston City Mission City Mission
    • St Vincent de Paul (Vinnies) Vinnies - Good Works | Vinnies
